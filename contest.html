H2I TikTok Contest Rules


Contest begins on April 15, 2021. Open to all enrolled 6th-12th grade students residing in the United States. Submissions must be received by 11:59pm on May 1, 2021. Individual and group submissions are welcome. Limit of five submissions from individuals/groups.

Participants must follow TikTok’s terms of service and privacy policy to be eligible. Parental consent must be obtained where necessary. Content must be written and created by named student participant(s) at time of entry. Participants may be disqualified if: a) participant is deemed to be in violation of these rules, b) if submission infringes or violates any rights of any third party, c) or if a participant acted in a manner that is inappropriate to include in the contest. Vulgar, racially, or ethnically insensitive content of any kind will not be tolerated and will result in participant’s immediate disqualification from the contest.

To enter:

  1. Select a meme from our Meme Pool at: Amazon Drive.
  2. Use the INFORM tools to discover examples of inaccurate information in your chosen meme.
  3. Make a TikTok video featuring the meme and your findings using INFORM to explain your discoveries. Show off your imagination and creativity!
  4. Follow H2I on TikTok (@how2inform) and Instagram (@how2inform).
  5. Tag H2I on TikTok (@how2inform) and at least three other TikTok accounts (individuals, groups, and/or organizations) of your choice.
  6. Add hashtags #H2I, #LewisUniversity, and at least three of the following of your choice: #H2INFORM #WordsMatter #MemeChallenge #DeconstructMemes #Deconstruction #Investigate #Identify #Narrative #Tone #Fact #Fiction #Opinion #Context #Reliable #Credible #Misinformation #Malinformation #Disinformation #ConspiracyTheory #VerifyB4UAmplify
  7. Share!

Winners will be selected by an internal voting process based on creativity and accurate use of INFORM to deconstruct selected meme in an original TikTok video, following H2I’s TikTok and Instagram accounts, tagging H2I on TikTok and at least three other active TikTok accounts, adding hashtags #H21, #LewisUniversity, and your pick of at least three of the following:

#H2INFORM #WordsMatter #MemeChallenge #DeconstructMemes #Deconstruction #Investigate #Identify #Narrative #Tone #Fact #Fiction #Opinion #Context #Reliable #Credible #Misinformation #Malinformation #Disinformation #ConspiracyTheory #VerifyB4UAmplify

Winners will receive prizes in the form of Amazon gift cards of varying amounts. A total of six prizes will be awarded. For group submissions: First place will receive a $250 gift card, second place will receive a $150 gift card, and third place will receive a $100 gift card. For individual submissions: First place will receive a $125 gift card, second place will receive a $100 gift card, and third place will receive a $75 gift card.

How2Inform will notify winners via direct messages to entrant’s TikTok and Instagram accounts by May 10, 2021. Response is necessary for delivery of prize. If winner(s) fail to respond within 5 business days of notification by How2Inform, prize(s) will be forfeited and alternative winner(s) will be selected. If no suitable winner(s), no prize(s) will be distributed.

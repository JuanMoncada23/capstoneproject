What is H2I?

H2I is How2Inform, which is a tool to fast-check online information. Use INFORM to deconstruct online content. INFORM.

Please click on INFORM to access more information.

What is Disinformation?

Disinformation: Is created to mislead, harm, or manipulate a person, a social group, an organization, or a country.

Please click on Disinformation to access more information.

What is Misinformation?

Misinformation: Is false information that has been disproven.

Please click on Misinformation to access more information.

What is Malinformation?

Malinformation: Is information based on fact, but taken out of
context to deliberately harm, manipulate, or mislead others.

Please click on Malinformation to access more information.

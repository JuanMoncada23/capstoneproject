What is H2I?

H2I stands for How2Inform. In which we are teaching young children
(such as yourself) how to fact check online information.

Misinformation

Misinformation - Is false information that has been disproven. The original author/creator may have
genuinely believed the information and shared it with good intentions.

Disinformation

Disinformation - Is created to mislead, harm, or manipulate a person, a social group, an organization, or a country

Malinformation

Malinformation - Is information based on fact, but taken out of context to deliberately harm, manipulate, or mislead others.
